About Me

Now based in Detroit, Michigan, I'm open to relocating for the right opportunity that offers growth and meaningful impact.

Professional Journey

My work spans from data analysis tools to automation pipelines for diesel emissions testing, always with a focus on creating solutions that people actually want to use. I'm currently expanding my skills in full-stack development during my free time while managing end-to-end project management at work.

Technical Focus

Currently, I'm diving deeper into full-stack development and exploring modern web frameworks to complement my automation and data analysis expertise.
